üìö How to Use the App

Step 1: Select a Dance Form

Click on one of the four dance options on the home screen.

Step 2: Choose a Pose

Select from the available poses and mudras (hand gestures) for that dance form.

Step 3: Read Instructions

Follow the step-by-step instructions provided for each pose.

Step 4: Start Webcam

Click the "Start Webcam" button to activate your camera.

Step 5: Record Pose

Stand in the correct pose and click "Record Pose" to save it as your target.

Step 6: Practice & Get Feedback

Try to match the recorded pose. The app shows your accuracy in real-time!

The green dots show your skeleton. Red dots are your current position, and the lines connect your joints.

üìä Understanding Pose Accuracy

The app compares your current pose with the recorded target pose:

  • 12 key joints are compared (shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles)
  • Accuracy is shown as a percentage (0-100%)
  • 75%+ = Great! You're matching the pose well
  • 50-75% = Good effort, keep refining
  • Below 50% = Try again, review the instructions
The more accurate your pose, the higher your percentage. Aim for 75%+ consistency!

üí° Tips for Better Pose Detection

Good Lighting: Natural light is best. Ensure your room is well-lit.
Camera Position: Position your camera at eye level, 1-2 meters away.
Clothing: Wear contrasting colors (dark clothes on light background or vice versa).
Background: Keep your background clear and uncluttered.
Full Body: Keep your entire body visible in the frame.
Practice Slowly: Move slowly and deliberately to perfect your form.

üîß Troubleshooting

‚ùå Webcam Not Working?

  • Ensure no other app is using the camera

  • Check the browser console (F12) for errors
‚ùå Poor Pose Detection?
  • Check your lighting conditions
  • Position yourself fully in frame
  • Wear contrasting colors
  • Move further from or closer to camera (1-2 meters ideal)
‚ùå Server Won't Start?
  • Make sure Python is installed
  • Check if port 8000 is available
  • Try a different port: python -m http.server 8080
If issues persist, check the browser console (F12) for detailed error messages.
