## ğŸ”Š Dance Forms Overview

### Bharatanatyam
- **Origin:** Tamil Nadu, South India
- **Focus:** Intricate footwork + hand gestures
- **Key Elements:** Aramandi stance, mudras, facial expressions

### Kathak
- **Origin:** North India (Uttar Pradesh)
- **Focus:** Storytelling + rapid footwork
- **Key Elements:** Pirouettes, hand gestures, rhythmic patterns

### Kathakali
- **Origin:** Kerala
- **Focus:** Elaborate costumes + expressive gestures
- **Key Elements:** Eye movements, hand mudras, elaborate makeup

### Odissi
- **Origin:** Odisha
- **Focus:** Curved movements + graceful gestures
- **Key Elements:** Tribhanga (three curves), hip movements, padasanchara
